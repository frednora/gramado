
Color scheme:

OS
│
├── Inside the Walls - WHITE
│   ├── R (Execution Hall)  - Red
│   │     • CPU faults & exceptions
│   │
│   ├── P (Peripheral Hall) - Pink
│   │     • IRQs & device signals
│   │
│   └── W (Request Hall)    - White
│         • syscalls & memory petitions
│
└── Outside the Wall - BROW 
│   ├── cmds/ - Brown
│   │     • POSIX-like programs
│   └──   • Utilities, shells
│
└── Outside the Wall - BLACK 
    └── apps/ - Black
          • Client-side GUI applications
