
 Entry sequency in Linux

The entry sequence is: 
startup_64 (ASM) >> x86_64_start_kernel (C) >> start_kernel (C).

The entry sequence is: 
startup_32 (ASM) >> i386_start_kernel (C) >> start_kernel (C).
